bar_chart Our Program Rating System

Pathway Canada uses an advanced calculation metric to assess your academic profile compatibility. We classify program alignments into four major tiers:

Locked In

Safety Fit

Your Top 6 average exceeds the program's target cutoff margin by **+3.0% or more**, with all prerequisite credits fully finalized.

verified High Likelihood
Strong Contender

Match Fit

Your admission average meets or falls within **-2.0% to +3.0%** of the program cutoff, and prerequisites are fully met.

bolt Steady Alignment
The Longshot

Reach Fit

Your average is **2.0% to 5.0% below** target cutoff. Admission is highly competitive but possible with portfolio weighting.

sailing Stretch Target
High Climb

Unlikely Fit

Your average is **more than 5.0% below** target cutoff, OR you are currently missing core senior prerequisite courses.

lock Prereqs Missing / Gap
