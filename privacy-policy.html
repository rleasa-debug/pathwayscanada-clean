Privacy & Data Sovereignty Policy

Effective Date: May 24, 2026. Last Updated: May 24, 2026.

1. Executive Overview: Data Sovereignty

Pathway Canada is committed to ensuring that Canadian high school students retain complete ownership over their academic data. In alignment with the **Consumer Privacy Protection Act (CPPA / Bill C-27)**, we implement data minimization, zero-password authentication, and structural isolation of student record databases to guarantee the highest level of cyber security.

shield The Zero-Password Architecture

Pathway Canada does not collect, record, or store passwords on our servers. All identity verification is delegated to secure enterprise OAuth providers (Google, Apple, and Facebook). This completely eliminates credential-based database exposure. If a data breach occurs, no user passwords exist to be compromised.

2. PII vs. Academic Record Isolation

To minimize cyber liability and ensure strict compliance with C-27 requirements, we enforce **strict data isolation**:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII), such as the student's legal name, date of birth, and social profile identifiers, are stored isolated from academic metrics.
  • Admissions Data & Grades are analyzed locally inside the user's browser runtime. When analytical telemetry is dispatched for platform optimization, all identifiers are scrubbed and anonymized, making it impossible to correlate individual grades to a specific legal identity.
  • Admissions Portfolios are hosted client-side by default, ensuring your grades are not centralized in a vulnerable public cloud database.

3. Right to Explanation (Explainable AI)

Under Canadian C-27 regulations, students have the right to request an explanation of any automated matching recommendation. We transparently publish our weighting logic:

  • Prerequisite Compliance (Hard Gate): Programs enforce specific prerequisite packages (e.g. senior physics, chemistry, or advanced math). Missing prerequisites will cap match likeness at 50% regardless of overall GPA.
  • Academic Average (GPA Weighting): Match ratings are scaled relative to institutional historical cutoffs. Average grades above the cutoff scale upward at a factor of 2.5x per point. Averages below cutoffs scale downward rapidly at a penalty factor of 5.0x per point.
  • Holistic Adjustments: Extracurricular and leadership activities are weighted qualitatively as additive confidence scores rather than hard entry caps.

4. User Control & Data Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)

Pathway Canada guarantees your right to delete your profile instantly:

All locally cached transcript and favorite records can be cleared by clicking the "Reset Portal" options on the dashboard or student settings pages. Any analytics logs are fully anonymized and carry no correlation identifiers to you.

5. Contact Information

For inquiries regarding our compliance or data processing, please contact the Pathways Canada Privacy and Security Officer at `privacy@pathwayscanada.ca`.
