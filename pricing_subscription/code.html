How does Pro billing work?

The $19 annual fee is billed once at the start of your subscription period. We do not offer monthly billing for individual student plans to keep administrative costs low for you.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade from Basic to Pro at any time during the academic cycle. Credits from your current period are applied automatically.

What is counselor access?

Pro members get access to a curated queue of certified academic advisors who can review your match results and provide 24-hour turnaround on specific admission questions.

Security & Stripe?

We never store your credit card information. All transactions are handled by Stripe, using bank-grade encryption to ensure your financial safety.
