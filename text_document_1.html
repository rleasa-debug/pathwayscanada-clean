# Technical Handoff: Chat & Stripe Integration Requirements To move from these high-fidelity designs to a fully functional live site, you will need to provide or configure the following: ## 1. Stripe (Payments) * **API Keys:** Once your Stripe account is active, you'll need to provide your **Publishable Key** for the frontend and keep your **Secret Key** for your backend server. * **Price ID:** Create the 'Pathway Canada Pro' product in your Stripe Dashboard and provide the **Price ID** (e.g., `price_1P...`). * **Webhook Secret:** If you want the site to automatically update student status after payment, you'll need to configure a webhook and provide the secret. ## 2. Chat Integration * **Service Provider:** Let me know which service you'd like to use (e.g., Intercom, Zendesk, Crisp, or a custom LLM solution). * **Integration Snippet/Key:** Most services provide a small JavaScript snippet or an **App ID/API Key**. Once you have that, I can 'embed' it into the designs. ## 3. ElevenLabs (Speech) * **API Key:** To enable the "Charlotte" voice and other audio features, you'll need to provide your **ElevenLabs API Key**. * **Voice ID:** If you have a specific custom voice ID for "Charlotte," please provide that as well. ## 4. Domain & Hosting * **Target URL:** Once you've decided on a domain (e.g., pathwaycanada.ca), let me know so I can ensure all internal links and security headers are correctly mapped. **Next Steps:** I have already integrated the *visual* elements for these. Once you have these keys/details, I can show you exactly where they go in the code!
